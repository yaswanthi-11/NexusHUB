About Nexus Hub

Nexus Hub was developed for the AI Model Development Contest 2026. Our mission is to bridge the gap between complex medical data and patient understanding using responsible AI.

Our Safety Approach

Unlike standard AI bots, we prioritize safety through:

  • Keyword Guardrails: Our system detects emergency red flags locally before sending data to any AI model.
  • Educational Tone: Our models (Gemini & OpenAI) are instructed to provide summaries, not diagnoses.
  • Privacy First: We do not store your medical reports; they are processed in-memory and discarded.

Technology Stack

This project leverages FastAPI for the backend and Multimodal Large Language Models (Gemini 1.5 Flash) for analyzing medical reports and images.
