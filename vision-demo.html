How it works

The system accepts images and PDFs, sends them to a vision model, and interprets structured medical values while preserving safety and non-diagnostic language.

Use case

For labs like CBC reports, the assistant can highlight abnormal values, explain trends, and encourage the user to share results with a clinician.
