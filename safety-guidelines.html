Red-flag protection

If the user mentions urgent symptoms, the assistant bypasses AI and immediately shows emergency instructions.

Non-diagnostic response

All AI-generated text uses a referral-first tone and avoids offering diagnosis or treatment instructions.

Visual support

Illustrations help presenters speak visually during a video without showing raw data or medical claims.
