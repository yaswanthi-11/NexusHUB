Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI work?

We use Gemini 1.5 Flash to parse medical text and images safely.

What kind of information can the AI analyze?

The AI can analyze text-based symptom descriptions, medical report images (like lab results), and PDF documents. It extracts key information and provides educational summaries.

Is my data stored?

No, all analysis is performed in-memory and discarded immediately.

How is my privacy protected?

Your privacy is paramount. Since data is not stored, there's no risk of it being accessed later. All processing happens in real-time and is ephemeral.

Can this replace a doctor?

Absolutely not. This is an educational tool for preliminary support only.

What happens if I enter emergency symptoms?

Our system has built-in emergency red-flag detection. If potentially urgent symptoms are detected, the AI is bypassed, and you are immediately directed to seek emergency medical care and provided with options to find nearby hospitals.

What languages does the assistant support?

The assistant primarily operates in English, but it can also detect and provide simulated responses in Hindi and Telugu for basic symptom descriptions.

How accurate are the AI summaries?

The AI aims to provide accurate educational summaries based on the input. However, it is an AI model and not a human doctor. Its output should always be verified by a medical professional.

Why do some responses say "Backend unavailable" or "Local simulation"?

This indicates that the connection to the AI model server might be temporarily down or your API keys are not correctly configured. In such cases, the system falls back to a safe, pre-programmed educational response.

Can I upload any type of file?

The system is designed to analyze image files (JPG, PNG, etc.) and PDF documents. Other file types are currently not supported for analysis.

What is the "Symptoms Library"?

The Symptoms Library is a collection of common medical symptoms with brief educational descriptions. You can click on a symptom to learn more and then analyze it on the Dashboard.

How does the "History" feature work?

The History page stores your recent analysis summaries locally in your browser's storage. This allows you to review past interactions. This data is not sent to our servers.

Can I download my analysis summaries?

Yes, after an analysis is complete, a "Download PDF Summary" button will appear, allowing you to save the educational summary as a PDF document to your device.

What is the purpose of the "Dark Mode" toggle?

The Dark Mode toggle allows you to switch the website's color scheme for better readability in low-light conditions or personal preference. Your preference is saved locally.

Who developed Nexus Hub?

Nexus Hub was developed as part of the AI Model Development Contest 2026, focusing on responsible AI in healthcare.
